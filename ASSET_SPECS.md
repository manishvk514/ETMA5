# Asset specs

## Hero background

Use either:

1. one premium background video plate, or
2. a sequence of 3 matched stills

### Video guidance
- 8 to 14 seconds
- 16:9 or wider
- very slow movement
- no fast montage
- rich blacks, controlled highlights
- avoid noisy compression

### Still guidance
- minimum width: 2200 px
- similar color grading between images
- each image should feel like part of one cinematic world

## Project thumbnails

Prepare one strong hero still per project first.

### Recommended sizes
- featured stills: 2200 px wide minimum
- archive cards: 1600 px wide minimum
- reel corridor: 1800 px wide minimum

## Thumbnail art direction

### JioStar
- panoramic, flagship, confident

### Hero Vida
- kinetic, harder light, more movement tension

### Krishna
- intimate, poised, quieter frame

### Surili Didi
- character-led, precise, slightly more playful

### Untitled
- atmospheric, spare, more mysterious

## Compression
- export jpg at high quality or optimized webp
- keep tonal richness in the shadows
- avoid over-sharpening
